About What Lagos Wore

What Lagos Wore is a fashion journalism platform capturing the rhythm, creativity, and unapologetic individuality that define Lagos style. We tell the stories behind what people wear from the quiet elegance of everyday looks to the bold statements that turn heads.

Our lens goes beyond trends. We explore the culture, emotion, and influence that shape fashion in Lagos and across Africa. Through stories, interviews, features, and visuals, we spotlight the people and brands shaping what it means to dress with intention, confidence, and edge.
At What Lagos Wore, we believe fashion is language and Lagos speaks fluently.
