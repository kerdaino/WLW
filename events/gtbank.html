GTCO Fashion Weekend 2025: Lagos Gets Ready for Style

Written by: Maryam Opoola

Lagos is already buzzing again. You can almost feel it in the air — fabrics being cut, designs coming to life, and fashion lovers getting ready for one of the city’s biggest weekends.

GTCO Fashion Weekend 2025 is around the corner, and this year promises to be even bigger. The event will take place on November 8th and 9th at the GT Centre in Victoria Island, Lagos.

GTCO Fashion Weekend isn’t just another fashion event — it’s a celebration of African style, talent, and enterprise. Since its launch in 2016, it’s been a hub for designers, stylists, and entrepreneurs to share ideas and redefine fashion narratives.

This year’s focus is on innovation, sustainability, and inclusion — the themes shaping global fashion conversations.

The GTCO Marketplace remains one of the most vibrant parts of the weekend. Here, upcoming designers and small fashion brands showcase and sell their collections directly to visitors — from ready-to-wear to handmade accessories.

It’s where the heartbeat of Lagos fashion lives: raw, vibrant, and full of stories.

The runway shows are always the highlight — blending tradition and modernity with reimagined indigenous fabrics and contemporary silhouettes.

Come early, wear your best street style, and capture the energy — because at GTCO Fashion Weekend, the people are part of the spectacle.

“This event reminds us what makes Lagos fashion special — its energy, creativity, and unstoppable drive.”
