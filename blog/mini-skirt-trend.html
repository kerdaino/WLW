Mini Skirt Trend

Published: Sept 2025 • Category: Front Row

Millennials are taking notes from Gen Z baddies on how to style the mini skirt, and the streets are buzzing with energy.

From Lagos Fashion Week to everyday street style, the mini skirt has evolved into more than just a throwback — it’s a bold statement piece.

Expect to see oversized blazers, knee-high boots, and fresh accessories redefining how the mini is worn.
