Pop-Up Showrooms

Published: Sept 2025 • Category: Events

Pop-up showrooms are becoming the new normal in Lagos, giving designers and brands a direct line to their audience.

These temporary spaces are raw, energetic, and alive with experimentation. From streetwear drops to luxury capsule collections, the city is hungry for it all.

If you missed this round, don’t worry — the pop-up wave is only getting started.
